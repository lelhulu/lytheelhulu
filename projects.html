Project 3: Banking System

Designed a comprehensive system for a local bank, incorporating user management for employees and customers. The system facilitates the tracking of various account types, including checking, saving, and CD accounts, with the flexibility for a customer to possess multiple accounts of each type. The implementation involves inheritance, where the "Account" class is subclassed into checking, saving, and CD account types, while the "Person" class is subclassed into employee and customer categories, providing a structured and modular approach to managing the bank's operations efficiently.
